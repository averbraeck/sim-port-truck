## Routes and Timing

Trucks have an origin and destination. These have to match the volumes and percentages from HVM 5.0. A simple way to start is to distribute all arriving and departing trucks according to HVM numbers. 

Trucks drive at a certain time of the day. These have to match the road usage tables of HVM 5.0. 

Deep-sea ships generate a certain volume. That volume has to be distributed across the day to not overstep the numbers from the timing tables in HVM. The origins and destinations from/to certain areas can be chosen from HVM.
